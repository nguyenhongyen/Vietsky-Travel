Why travel with Steppes.

Sustainability at our core

We’re a Certified B Corp meaning that when you choose to travel with us, you’re choosing to help protect what matters.
Our Fund for Change programme supports community and sustainability projects and local charities across the globe and we offset carbon emissions from your flights. We insist our on-ground partners work to reduce their use of single-use plastics. And because protecting our planet is part of everything we do, you can travel in the knowledge that you’re contributing to its wellbeing with every step of your journey.
