A love affair with travel

For over thirty years we’ve put everything into making sure our knowledge, expertise and passion combine to create extraordinary travel experiences for our clients, wherever in the world they want to go. We’re perfectly placed to deliver holidays tailored to your requirements, bolstered by our experience, understanding and respect for local customs, the ‘ins and outs’ of the world’s destinations and for our planet.

Unrivalled expertise

Our people are our brilliance. Our globally recognised team of Travel Experts live and breathe their destinations, continuously exploring, learning and refining their knowledges to provide clients with insight and expertise born from real experiences. Speaking to them provides an immediate and intimate connection with destination and accommodation that brochures cannot match; curating and suggesting travel experiences that’ll ignite your passions and turn new and long held dreams into reality.

A team you can trust

We’re with you every step of the way. Steppes Travellers explore every corner of our planet and, in an often-changing world, getting you there and bringing you home are always foremost in our thinking and dedication and support underpin the reason why 70% of our business is referral or repeat. Should you want to change your itinerary before you go, or should something unexpected happen whilst you’re away, we’re on hand 24/7 to support you, allowing you to enjoy an assurance that standard holiday bookings cannot bring.
